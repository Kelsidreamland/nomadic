# Design Philosophy: Soft Pastel (柔和粉彩)

**Name the movement**: "Soft Pastel"

**Philosophy**:
"Soft Pastel" captures the ephemeral joy of a sunrise flight or the gentle haze of a new city at dawn. It is an aesthetic movement defined by light, air, and effortless grace. Unlike heavy, grounded designs, this philosophy embraces buoyancy and optimism, communicating a sense of ease and delightful discovery. It is an interface that feels like a gentle breeze.

Space and form here are expansive and light. Elements appear to float effortlessly within the composition, separated by vast, breathing negative space. Forms are inherently soft, utilizing pill-shaped buttons and circular motifs that invite interaction. The spatial arrangement is airy, requiring master-level execution to maintain structure without feeling rigid. Every element is meticulously crafted to feel weightless yet perfectly balanced.

The color palette is a masterclass in subtlety: creamy whites (`#FDFBF7`), soft peach (`#FAD2B8`), muted lavender (`#E8D7E0`), and warm sunlight yellow (`#F9E4A6`). These colors blend and interact softly, creating a luminous environment that reduces cognitive load. Information is encoded through these gentle chromatic shifts, making the interface feel less like a tool and more like a serene digital sanctuary.

Typography in "Soft Pastel" is delicate and highly refined. Thin, elegant font weights are prioritized, allowing the words to breathe alongside the visual elements. Text is minimal, serving only to gently guide the user through the spatial expression. The resulting composition is a product of deep expertise, where painstaking attention to tracking and leading ensures the typographic voice remains a soft, reassuring whisper within the overarching visual joy.
